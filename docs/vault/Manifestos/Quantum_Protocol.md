# Nu Flow Quantum Protocol

> "Classical makes it true. Quantum makes it usable."

## 1. Classical Truth, Quantum Foresight
We do not quantum-ize truth. We quantum-ize proposals, predictions, and plans.
*   **Classical Ledger**: The river that did flow. Append-only, strict, truth.
*   **Quantum Layer**: The cloud system that might rain. Probabilistic, adaptive.

## 2. New Objects
### 2.1 ProposedFlowEvent (Superposition)
A candidate truth with probability mass across multiple routes.
*   **Superposition**: "Lunch with Sarah" could be `S6 Social` (30%) or `S6 Work` (70%).
*   **Status**: `open`, `collapsed`, `expired`.

### 2.2 CollapseEvent (Measurement)
Daily Close is a **Measurement Ceremony**.
*   Collapses proposals into truth.
*   Turns uncertainty into clean ledger entries.

### 2.3 ScenarioBranch (Many Futures)
Week planning creates Branch A ("Ship Mode"), Branch B ("Recovery"), etc.
*   **Simulated**: Deltas, reservoir levels, backpressure growth.
