# Life Focus: The 9 Pillars

> Your 9 focus "worlds" that cover the complete ontology of human experience.

## The 9 Pillars

| Code | Focus | Description | Archetype |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **F01** | **CORE** | Identity, Faith, Purpose. "Being." (Ob1) | The Soul |
| **F02** | **SELF** | Heart, Body, Mind. "Internal OS." (Ob2) | The Vessel |
| **F03** | **CIRCLE** | Relationships, Family, Friends. (R1) | The Connector |
| **F04** | **GRIND** | Work, Responsibilities, Economic Engine. (Neh1) | The Builder |
| **F05** | **LEVEL UP** | Skills, Business (Nu), Growth. (Neh2) | The Architect |
| **F06** | **IMPACT** | Giving back, Nature, Community. (Dut1) | The Gardener |
| **F07** | **PLAY** | Creativity, Travel, Joy. (Rec1) | The Explorer |
| **F08** | **INSIGHT** | Knowledge, Philosophy, Systems Thinking. (Rec2) | The Sage |
| **F09** | **CHAOS** | The Unexpected, Crisis, Opportunity. (New) | The Storm |

## Why 9?
The universe is 8 structured domains + 1 force (Chaos) that distorts all structure.
Every story is a character (Self) in a world (Context) with relationships (Circle), working (Grind), aspiring (Level Up), seeking joy (Play), gaining wisdom (Insight), and facing chaos (Chaos).
