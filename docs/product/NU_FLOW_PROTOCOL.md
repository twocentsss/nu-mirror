## Personalization: Managing "Me"
The Nu Platform offers the engine (The Ledger, The Graph). **You bring the rules.**

### 1. Your Data, Your Rules (BYO)
We don't want your data. We want you to own it. We just orchestrate the magic.
*   **BYO Keys**: Encrypt your private data with your own keys. We never see your raw thoughts.
*   **BYO Storage**: S3, Google Drive, or local. Your life, your cloud.
*   **BYO Config**: Fork the config sheet and run your own rules. Full control.
