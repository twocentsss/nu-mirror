# Apple Style Design Protocol

## Philosophy
The Apple Style is visionary, high-contrast, and focused on "The Stance." it is designed to wow the user, using scale and motion to convey premium quality and a clear behavioral shift. It's the style for landing pages, protocol explains, and "Why Wait?" provocations.

## Visual Language
- **Backgrounds**: Deep, absolute blacks (`bg-black`) or high-end solid grays.
- **Gradients**: Sophisticated linear and radial gradients (e.g., `from-white via-white to-white/40`) used for text and cards.
- **Typography Tokens**: 
  - Main Heading: `text-6xl sm:text-7xl font-extrabold tracking-tighter leading-[1.05]`
  - Eyebrow: `text-[14px] font-bold uppercase tracking-[0.6em] text-blue-500`
  - Sub-label: `text-[10px] font-black uppercase tracking-[0.3em]`
- **Color Palettes (Reel Gradients)**:
  - Blue: `from-blue-600 to-indigo-600`
  - Purple: `from-purple-600 to-pink-600`
  - Emerald: `from-emerald-600 to-teal-600`
  - Orange: `from-orange-600 to-red-600`
- **Shapes**: Experimental, large-radius corners (`rounded-[2.5rem]`, `rounded-[4rem]`).

## Components
- **Stance Cards**: High-contrast, gradient-backed cards that anchor a life stage or specific promise.
- **The Reel**: Infinite horizontal scrolling tickers of "ads" with high-vibrancy gradients and smooth hover-to-reveal interactions.
- **Jobs-ian Footer**: Centered, minimalistic, with strong call-to-action buttons and elegant top-border gradients.

## Motion
- **Entry**: Subtle vertical translations (`translate-y-4` to `0`) and opacity fades.
- **Interactions**: Scale-up on hover (`hover:scale-[1.02]`), smooth transitions (`duration-500`).
- **Continuous**: Infinite loops for ambient motion (like the Promotional Reel).

## Usage
Use this style for:
- `/how-to`
- Landing pages
- Feature introductions
- Any moment requiring high "semantic weight" and inspiration.
